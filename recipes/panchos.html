Panchos

Recipe

After the boom of hamburgers and hamburger joints, it is now time for hot dogs, a food as noble as it is versatile. The pancho is now treated with respect, care and reversion to transform that simple sausage in a vienna bread into a complete and complex meal.

Ingredients:

  • Vienna sausages
  • Bread for pancho
  • Coarse salt
  • Water
  • Optional toppings

Steps

  1. In a pot we put the handful of salt. Add half of the water and bring to a boil.
  2. While the water is boiling, cut the bread in half. It is important not to throw the bags away.
  3. Once boiling, place the sausages in the water.
  4. We wait approximately 3 minutes and remove the sausages and place them in the buns.
