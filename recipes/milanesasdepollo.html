Milanesas de pollo

Recipe

Preparing a perfect chicken milanesa, like the ones your grandmother/aunt/mother used to make is not as complicated as you think. It's all a matter of following certain steps and knowing some secrets so that the flavor and texture are incredible.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken supreme
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds and flax seeds
  • 2 eggs
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Spices (oregano, ground chili bell pepper, mustard seeds), optional

Steps

  1. In a bowl or deep dish, beat the eggs. Season with salt and pepper and season to taste with spices.
  2. Fillet the chicken breast, to obtain 4 milanesas.
  3. Leave the chicken fillets in the egg mixture in the refrigerator for two hours.
  4. In a bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with the seeds.
  5. Bread the fillets by coating them with the bread mixture. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  6. Fry in abundant oil. Place on absorbent paper and serve with salad.
